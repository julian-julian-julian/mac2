Macbeth is the titular character, an old scottish nobleman who earned his title trough distinquished action during wartime. He recives a phrofecy promising him kinghood.

From the rise to the throne of Macbeth, shrouded in visions and madness caused by her regrets, Lady Macbeth sees her husband begin a violent purge in his own castle. After a sleepwalking scene in which she is also tormented, Lady Macbeth ends up committing suicide.
